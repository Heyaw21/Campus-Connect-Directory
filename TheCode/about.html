The Campus Connect Directory is a student project designed to help AASTU students explore, connect, and participate in campus communities. It provides a comprehensive list of associations and clubs along with Telegram links to join and stay updated.

This platform helps students enhance skills, collaborate with peers, and engage in professional and creative activities. From workshops and mentorship programs to social and charity events, this directory provides everything you need to make the most of campus life.
